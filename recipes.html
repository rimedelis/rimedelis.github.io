Recipes

Chicken Fajitas

6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 3 chicken breasts 🐔
  • 1 onion 🧅
  • Salt 🧂
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Olive oil 🫒
  • 1 red bell pepper 🌶️
  • 1 yellow bell pepper 🌶️
  • 1 green bell pepper 🌶️
  • Sour cream (if available) 🍶
  • 6 tortillas 🌮
  • Lemon 🍋
  • Cheddar cheese 🧀
  • Leek or scallions
  • Cilantro 🌿

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken into cubes and season with salt, cumin, and paprika.
  2. Heat a skillet.
  3. Cut the onion and peppers into strips.
  4. Add olive oil to the skillet and add the chicken, onion, and peppers. Season with salt and sauté.
  5. Turn off the heat and let it cook in the hot skillet.
  6. In each tortilla, add sour cream, chicken with vegetables, sautéed scallions, cheese, lemon juice, and cilantro.
  7. Fold the tortilla and enjoy.

Rice and Salmon

2 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon steaks 🐟
  • 2 cloves of garlic, sliced 🧄
  • Olive oil 🫒

Ingredients (Rice):

  • 1/2 onion 🧅
  • 1 garlic clove 🧄
  • 1 carrot 🥕
  • 150 g white rice 🍚
  • Salt 🧂
  • Herbes de Provence
  • Olive oil 🫒

Instructions:

  1. For the rice, in a saucepan, add water, onion, garlic, chopped carrot, salt, and olive oil. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add the rice, and just before turning off the heat, add the Herbes de Provence.
  3. Five minutes before the rice is done, heat a skillet with olive oil and garlic over low heat for two minutes.
  4. Add the salmon steaks and cook until they are golden brown.

Mushroom and Veggies Risotto

2 servings

Ingredients:

  • Risotto rice (150 g) 🍚
  • Mushrooms 🍄
  • Lactose-free cheese 🧀
  • Olive oil 🫒
  • Salt 🧂
  • Assorted vegetables
  • 1/2 onion 🧅
  • Dried thyme 🌿
  • 2 cloves of garlic 🧄

Instructions:

  1. In a deep non-stick pan, add water, olive oil, onion, garlic, and sautéed/cut vegetables. Bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add the rice, salt, mushrooms, and let it cook.
  3. When it's done, turn off the heat and add the cheese and thyme. Stir well and serve.

Bean Rice with Tomato Pulp

2 servings

Ingredients:

For the bean with tomato pulp:

  • Butter beans
  • Tomato pulp 🍅
  • Garlic and onion for sautéing 🧄🧅
  • Thyme 🌿
  • Cheese from São Jorge Island 🧀

For the rice:

  • White rice 🍚
  • Onion and garlic 🧅🧄
  • Salt 🧂
  • Vegetable broth (Knorr)

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, sauté garlic, onion, butter beans, thyme, and tomato pulp. Add the São Jorge cheese to the tomato pulp pan as well.
  2. In a separate pot, prepare the rice with onion, garlic, salt, and vegetable broth.
  3. Combine the bean mixture with the rice in individual plates when serving.

Oatmeal Porridge

1 serving

Ingredients:

  • Water 🚰
  • Steel-cut oats 🥣
  • Sliced banana 🍌
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Peanut butter 🥜

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and add steel-cut oats. As the oats are cooking, add sliced banana and a dollop of peanut butter. Cook until reaching the desired consistency.
  2. Top with additional sliced banana, ground cinnamon, and more peanut butter, if desired.
  3. Stir well and enjoy!

Pasta with Fish and Yogurt Sauce

2 servings

Ingredients:

For the fish:

  • Garlic and onion for sautéing 🧄🧅
  • Fish fillets 🐟
  • Salt for seasoning 🧂
  • Thyme and rosemary 🌿
  • Olive oil 🫒

For the pasta:

  • Pasta
  • Salt 🧂

For the yogurt sauce:

  • Natural yogurt 🍦
  • Parsley 🌿
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon juice 🍋
  • Cilantro 🌿

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and cook fish with salt, thyme, rosemary, and olive oil.
  2. Cook pasta with salt.
  3. Prepare yogurt sauce by mixing yogurt, parsley, black pepper, lemon juice, and cilantro.
  4. Combine pasta, fish, and top with yogurt sauce. Grate cheese over the dish. Serve and enjoy!

Halal Cart Chicken & Rice with Garlic Sauce

2 servings

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • Chicken breasts 🐔
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Garlic 🧄
  • Chili powder
  • Salt 🧂
  • Olive oil 🫒
  • Lemon juice 🍋

For the rice:

  • Normal rice
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Garlic and salt 🧄🧂
  • Turmeric

For the sauce:

  • Natural yogurt 🍦
  • Garlic 🧄
  • Cilantro 🌿
  • Cumin
  • Salt 🧂
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon juice 🍋

Instructions:

  1. Cook the chicken with oregano, cumin, paprika, garlic, chili powder, salt, olive oil, and lemon juice on high heat for 6-8 minutes.
  2. Cook the rice with cumin, paprika, garlic, salt, and turmeric. Add water and let it cook until golden brown.
  3. Prepare the sauce by mixing natural yogurt, garlic, cilantro, cumin, salt, black pepper, and lemon juice until smooth and creamy.
  4. Ideally, serve with a salad.

Mó's Poke Bowl with Honey

1 serving

Ingredients:

For the bowl:

  • Avocado 🥑
  • Raw salmon 🐟
  • Sweet and sour sauce
  • Rice 🍚
  • Soy sauce
  • Mayonnaise 🍶
  • Honey 🍯

For the rice:

  • Boiled rice with salt 🍚🧂
  • Optional: Onion or garlic for flavor 🧅🧄

Instructions:

  1. Mix together avocado, raw salmon, sweet and sour sauce, rice, soy sauce, mayonnaise, and honey.
  2. For the rice, simply boil it with salt and optionally add a bit of onion or garlic for flavor.
  3. Combine the rice with the rest of the ingredients and enjoy fresh.
